(BASE DMG) * (1+DMG BONUS)  * (1+CRITDMG)) * (1-RESISTANCE) * (1 - ELEMENT ADV) * (DEF) * (1 - (ENEMYLVL - LVL) * 0.01)


BASE DMG = (BASE ATK + (BASE ATK * ATK%)) * (SKILL MULTIPLIER + EXTRA MULTIPLIER)
DEF = (5000 - (ENEMY DEF - (ENEMYDEF * (DEF REDUC + DEF PEN))))/5000



ATK% - all atk bonuses
DMG BONUS - literaly dmg bonus
CRIT DMG - critical damage
RESISTANCE - enemy resistance (100% - 0%)
ELEMENT ADV - refer to chart
ENEMY DEF - the amount of defense the enemy posseses (Caps at 4000)
DEF REDUC - reduces enemy defense (100% - 0%)
